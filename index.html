Our Services

Peter Raarup Landscape Design is a professional practice of landscape architecture based in New Orleans, Louisiana. Since 1991, the firm has been designing and installing residential and commercial landscapes ranging in scale from French Quarter courtyards to expansive estates and office complexes.

Our Company

Peter Raarup, a licensed Landscape Architect, Horticulturist, & Landscape Contractor, established Peter Raarup Landscape Design, inc., in 1991. He graduated with a B. S. in biology from Tulane University and completed his Master of Landscape Architecture at Louisiana State University.

Peter’s many commercial landscape projects can be seen in the Greater New Orleans area, Baton Rouge and Pensacola, Florida, as well as multiple roof gardens, terraces, and courtyards in New Orleans and New York City.

Being civic minded, Peter also designed and installed several of the gardens of Lazarus House, a residence for persons with AIDS, in New Orleans.

The press likes Peter Raarup Landscape Design, and you may see articles written on his knowledge in the landscape field. Peter appeared in two HDTV landscape design episodes, and was the subject of a major article in the “New Orleans” magazine, homes and gardens section.

See more of Peter Raarup landscape examples on HOUZZ, wherein he is a multi-year influencer.

Peter is the principle and owner of Peter Raarup Landscape Design and is responsible for all aspects of the company's business.
