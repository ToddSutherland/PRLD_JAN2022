Design

Peter Raarup Landscape Design offers a complete spectrum of design services for all sizes of projects. We work directly with each client to develop a design that fulfills their personal ideals, while addressing the functional needs of the project, and working within the budget. Our firm works with the latest AutoCAD program to facilitate and expedite cooperation with architects, engineers, and builders.

We develop schematics, master plans, and complete construction documents. We also provide full color presentation renderings upon request.
